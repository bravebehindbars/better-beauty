Skin & Hair That Matters

Better Hygiene For Incarcerated Men And Women

Millions of incarcerated men and women do not have access to quality beauty and hygiene products. The options available are extremely limited and subpar in comparison with what is available to the average citizen.

Our mission at Better Beauty is to bring awareness to the benefits that quality beauty and hygiene items could have on both the physical and mental health of incarcerated men and women.

When You Look Good You Feel Good!!

Self-Confidence is important to EVERYONE, and the conditon of our hair, skin, and teeth has a significant impact on how we view ourselves. Incarcerated people NEED confidence to overcome the various challenges they face in prison as well as the ones they will face upon release.

SKIN CARE

You may already know that healthy skin helps you look your best.

But it does so much more that that!

Did you know your skin is your body's largest organ? Good skin care is linked to better overall health, both physical and mental.

HAIR CARE

Healthy hair is critical to both your physical and mental well-being and should be taken seriously. With the proper maintenance and care, it is possible to have healthy, vibrant hair at all times. Furthermore, your overall confidence will be significantly improved and allows you to live a more fulfilling life.

ORAL CARE

Good oral hygiene has many benefits including:

  • Healthier Gums
  • Reduced Risk For Heart Attack
    • Healthier Lungs
  • Decreased Cancer Risk
Remember, when you take care of your teeth, you’re doing more than preventing cavaties- you’re protecting your overall health.
